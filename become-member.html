Message From The Vicar - Rev. Emmah Omondi

“Please allow me to cordially welcome you to the ACK ALL SAINTS MASENO PARISH, in the Diocese of Maseno South. We are a one-Church parish located along the Kisumu - Busia Road at Maseno Municipality, in the neighborhood of Maseno University and adjacent to Maseno School for Hearing Impaired.

We are committed to following Jesus and joyously worshiping Him. Our Theme for 2025 is from John 4:29: ‘Come, see a man…’

As a church family, we seek to be an exemplary congregation bound together by the love of Christ, reaching and touching lives by word and deed. Feel free to worship with us and participate in our activities.”
