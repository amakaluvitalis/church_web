Our Ministries

At ACK All Saints Maseno Parish, we have various ministries that cater to different groups within our church, fostering spiritual growth, community service, and fellowship. Find a ministry that suits you and get involved!

Sunday School

Teaching children the Word of God through Bible lessons, songs, and activities to nurture their faith.

Boys and Girls Brigade

Instilling Christian values, leadership skills, and discipline through structured activities.

Teens Ministry

A safe space for teenagers to grow in faith and develop strong Christian values.

Youth Ministry

Empowering young adults to live out their faith and engage in outreach.

Praise and Worship Ministry

Leading the congregation in spirit-filled worship through music and prayer.

Choir Ministry

Enhancing worship through melodious choral music and performances.

Mothers’ Union

A fellowship for women committed to strengthening Christian families, empowering women, and engaging in charity work.

Anglican Christian Women Fellowship (ACWF)

Bringing together women in faith to support one another, participate in church programs, and nurture spiritual growth.

Scheme Fellowships

Small fellowship groups within different residential areas, where members meet for prayers, Bible study, and mutual support.

Kenya Anglican Men Association (KAMA)

Encouraging Christian men to take up leadership roles in their families, church, and community.

Get Involved

Each ministry plays a vital role in our church community. If you feel called to serve, join any of our ministries and be part of our mission.
