Administration

Meet our dedicated leaders who guide and manage the Anglican Church of Kenya. Together, they ensure the spiritual, administrative, and operational success of our church.

The Big Four

Rev. John Mwangi

Archbishop

Archbishop John Mwangi oversees the spiritual vision and leadership of the Anglican Church, guiding us with faith and integrity.

Canon Grace Atieno

Bishop

Bishop Grace Atieno leads the Diocese with compassion and wisdom, ensuring growth and development within the church.

Rev. Peter Kamau

Dean

Dean Peter Kamau focuses on liturgical excellence and theological education, nurturing the faith of our congregation.

Elder Sarah Nyambura

Church Secretary

Sarah Nyambura manages church records and communications, ensuring smooth operations within the organization.

Senior Leaders

Rev. George Ochieng

Youth Pastor

George Ochieng ministers to the youth, providing guidance and mentorship to inspire the next generation.

Ms. Margaret Achieng

Treasurer

Margaret Achieng oversees church finances with integrity, ensuring transparency and accountability in financial matters.

Elder Patrick Otieno

Music Director

Patrick Otieno leads the music ministry, cultivating an atmosphere of worship and praise in the church.

Ms. Rose Nyakio

Event Coordinator

Rose Nyakio coordinates church events, ensuring they are meaningful, organized, and impactful.

Church Committee

Rev. James Onyango

Committee Chair

James Onyango chairs the church committee, ensuring efficient planning and decision-making.

Ms. Faith Mutua

Outreach Coordinator

Faith Mutua oversees community outreach programs, ensuring the church remains a beacon of hope.

Elder Simon Wanjiru

Facilities Manager

Simon Wanjiru manages church facilities, ensuring they are clean, safe, and welcoming.

Ms. Lucy Wambui

Prayer Coordinator

Lucy Wambui leads the prayer ministry, fostering spiritual growth and unity in the church.
