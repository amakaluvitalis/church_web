Periodic Activities

Community Outreach Program

Our community outreach program aimed to support underprivileged families by providing food supplies, clothes, and essential services. It was a great opportunity to spread kindness and love in the community.

Annual Youth Camp

The annual youth camp was a time of spiritual growth, fun activities, and deep fellowship. We encouraged young members to develop their faith and leadership skills while enjoying nature.

Annual Choir Concert

Our choir concert brought together voices in worship and celebration, showcasing the incredible talent within our church. It was a night of praise and thanksgiving with uplifting music.

Weekly Bible Study Groups

We organize weekly Bible study sessions to deepen our understanding of the Word of God and foster meaningful discussions among members.

Charity Fundraiser Event

Our charity event successfully raised funds for our mission projects, supporting various humanitarian causes. It was an amazing day filled with generosity and community spirit.

Children's Sunday School

Our Sunday School program focuses on teaching children biblical principles in a fun and engaging way, helping them grow in faith from an early age.
